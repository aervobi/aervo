How it works

From scattered data to a clear view

Aervo gives you a simple three step setup that turns all your separate tools into one live control center for your business.

1. Connect your data

Link your store, payments, and accounting tools in a few clicks. No engineering degree required.

2. Train your AI view

Tell Aervo what matters most, such as inventory risk, profit margin, or repeat customers, and it tunes your dashboard around those.

3. Act on live insights

Get a live control center with alerts, summaries, and clear next steps so you can make decisions with confidence.
