What is AERVO?

AERVO is my vision for a 360-degree assistant for small businesses. It connects to the tools they already use and turns raw numbers into clear answers: “How did I do today? What’s selling? What’s running low? What should I worry about next?”

Why I'm building this

Most small business owners are busy surviving the day. They shouldn't have to be data experts to understand how their business is really doing. I want AERVO to feel like a smart, calm partner that looks over everything and says: “Here’s what happened, here’s what changed, and here’s what to do.”

About AERVO

AERVO is built on a simple idea: small business owners deserve clarity. Running a business means juggling sales, inventory, trends, customers, and daily challenges. Most people don’t have time to dig through spreadsheets or dashboards to understand what’s actually happening day to day.

AERVO is designed to give a calm, high-level view of everything that matters. It takes real business data and turns it into clear answers, simple summaries, and meaningful insights. The goal isn’t to overwhelm people — the goal is to give them confidence and control.

AERVO helps business owners see the big picture so they can make decisions with clarity instead of stress.
