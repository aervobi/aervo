Promise to myself

This page is my reminder: I am capable of learning, building, and figuring things out one step at a time. AERVO doesn't need to be perfect today. I just need to keep moving, keep experimenting, and not give up on the version of me who believed this could work.

When things feel heavy, I will remember why I started: to create something that gives small business owners clarity, confidence, and a calmer way to understand their numbers.

I will be patient with myself, stay curious, and have the courage to keep going, even when the path feels unclear.

Keep going. Future me is proud of this.
