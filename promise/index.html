Promise to myself

This page is my reminder that I am capable of learning, building, and figuring things out one step at a time. AERVO does not have to be perfect today. What matters is that I keep moving, keep experimenting, and don’t walk away from the version of me who believed this could work.

When things feel heavy, I’ll remember why I started: to create something that gives small business owners clarity, confidence, and a calmer way to understand their numbers.

I will be patient with myself, stay curious, and have the courage to keep going even when the path feels unclear. Every small step counts.

Keep going. Future me is proud of this.
